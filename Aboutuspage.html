About Us


Our mission is to help readers to cook with simplicity and pleasure, using the ingredients you have at home.
For us, what matters is inspiring you to be hands on in the kitchen!
We are a community of food lovers and the idea is to create a collaborative website, a kind of shared cookbook that brings together the traditional family cookies and new discoveries in the kitchen.
