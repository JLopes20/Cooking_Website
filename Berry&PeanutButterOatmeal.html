Berry & Peanut Butter Oatmeal

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk of choice (dairy, coconut or almond)
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 1 1/2 cups rolled oats
  • pinch of salt
  • toppings: sliced banana, sliced cherries, peanut butter, raspberries & blueberries

Directions

  1. Bring milk and water to a simmer, add oats & salt and bring to a boil.
    Reduce heat to a simmer and cook, stirring occasionally for about 5 minutes
  2. Add toppings of choice

Enjoy!
