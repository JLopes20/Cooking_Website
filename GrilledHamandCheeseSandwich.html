Grilled Ham and Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole grain bread
  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 2 slices Swiss cheese
  • 2 thin slices deli ham
  • 1 teaspoon mayonnaise(optional)
  • 1 teaspoon whole grain mustard(optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat a skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Spread one side of each slice of bread with 1 teaspoon butter. Place one slice, butter-side
    down in the hot skillet. Top with Swiss cheese and ham. Spread the unbuttered
    side of the second slice of bread with mayonnaise and mustard; place it, butter-side
    up on top of the sandwich. Cook until the sandwich is golden brown and the cheese is melted, about 3 minutes per side.

Enjoy!
